Bring back print menus

Thu 28 October 2021

Print menus are great. A lot of effort and thought usually goes into the menu organization and presentation. When I load a menu from a QR code, I often get a pdf. I find a pdf awkward to navigate. It's not even the bad menu user experience that I care about the most. I just want one less excuse to open my phone.

Bring back print menus.
